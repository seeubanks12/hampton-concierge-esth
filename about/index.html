About Dr. Hampton

Kate Hampton has been a practicing nurse for over fifteen years with the Veterans Affairs hospital. After receiving her Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN) from Xavier University, she studied in the prestigious U.S. Army Graduate Program in Anesthesia Nursing, earning her Doctor of Nursing Practice degree and qualifying as a Certified Registered Nurse Anesthetist (DNP, CRNA). As Dr. Hampton, she has been serving the Memphis veterans population since 2018. She is a proud member of the American Association of Facial Esthetics, earning certifications in botulinum (aka Botox) and dermal fillers through their national network. She lives in Midtown Memphis with her husband of 10 years and their son.
